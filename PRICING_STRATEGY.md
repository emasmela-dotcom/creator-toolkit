## 2. Seller Tool Pricing Guidelines

### Pricing Models

#### A. One-Time Purchase
**Best for:**
- Templates
- Checklists
- Generators
- Downloadable resources
- No ongoing maintenance

**Price Range: $5 - $50**
- Simple templates: $5-$15
- Comprehensive tools: $15-$30
- Premium packages: $30-$50

**Examples:**
- Email signature generator: $9
- Content calendar template: $12
- Launch checklist: $15
- Complete template pack: $29

#### B. Monthly Subscription
**Best for:**
- Hosted tools
- Regular updates
- Analytics tools
- Automation tools
- Management tools

**Price Range: $5 - $50/month**
- Basic tools: $5-$10/month
- Standard tools: $10-$20/month
- Premium tools: $20-$35/month
- Enterprise tools: $35-$50/month

**Examples:**
- Simple CRM: $12/month
- Content scheduler: $15/month
- Analytics dashboard: $19/month
- AI content writer: $29/month

#### C. Yearly Subscription
**Best for:**
- Same as monthly, but with discount

**Discount: 20% off monthly**
- $10/month = $96/year (save $24)
- $20/month = $192/year (save $48)
- $30/month = $288/year (save $72)

**Benefits:**
- Better cash flow for sellers
- Lower churn
- Higher customer lifetime value

#### D. Usage-Based (Future)
**Best for:**
- Heavy computation
- API-heavy tools
- Per-use services

**Examples:**
- $0.10 per API call
- $1 per 1000 operations
- $5 per report generated

## 4. Pricing Psychology

### Price Points That Convert

**Sweet Spots:**
- **$9, $12, $15** - Most common for monthly
- **$19, $24, $29** - Premium monthly
- **$5, $9, $15** - One-time purchases

**Why:**
- Under $10 feels affordable
- $12-$15 is "reasonable"
- $19-$29 is "premium but accessible"
- Over $30 needs strong value prop

### Pricing Strategies

#### 1. Anchor Pricing
Show higher price first, then discount:
- ~~$29/month~~ ‚Üí $19/month (Save 34%)

#### 2. Value-Based Pricing
Price based on value delivered:
- Saves 5 hours/week = $50/month
- Increases revenue 20% = $100/month

#### 3. Competitive Pricing
Price relative to competitors:
- Competitor: $30/month
- You: $24/month (20% less, same features)

#### 4. Freemium Model (Optional)
- Free: Basic features
- Paid: Advanced features
- Good for discovery

## 11. Pricing Optimization Tips

### Test Different Prices

1. **Start higher, lower if needed**
   - Easier to lower than raise
   - Higher price = better quality perception

2. **A/B test pricing**
   - Test $15 vs $19
   - See which converts better

3. **Monitor conversion rates**
   - If low conversions, price might be too high
   - If high conversions, might be too low

4. **Seasonal pricing**
   - Launch discounts
   - Black Friday sales
   - New Year promotions

5. **Bundle pricing**
   - Encourage multiple purchases
   - Better value perception

## 12. Common Pricing Mistakes

### ‚ùå Don't:

1. **Price too low**
   - Devalues your tool
   - Attracts wrong customers
   - Unsustainable

2. **Price too high**
   - Low conversions
   - Fewer customers
   - Hard to justify

3. **No yearly option**
   - Missing revenue opportunity
   - Higher churn

4. **Unclear pricing**
   - Confuses buyers
   - Reduces conversions

5. **Set and forget**
   - Market changes
   - Competitors change
   - Need to adjust

### ‚úÖ Do:

1. **Research competitors**
2. **Test different prices**
3. **Show value clearly**
4. **Offer yearly discount**
