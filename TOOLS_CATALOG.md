## Pricing Models

Most tools will use one of these pricing models:

1. **One-time purchase** ($5-$50)
   - Templates, checklists, generators
   - No ongoing maintenance needed

2. **Monthly subscription** ($5-$30/month)
   - Tools requiring hosting or regular updates
   - Analytics, automation, management tools

3. **Yearly subscription** (20% discount on monthly)
   - Same as monthly but with annual commitment

4. **Usage-based** (for some tools)
   - Pay per use for heavy computation tools

## Tool Requirements

Each tool should:
- ✅ Solve ONE specific problem
- ✅ Be usable by non-technical creators
- ✅ Have a simple, intuitive interface
- ✅ Work immediately (no complex setup)
- ✅ Be mobile-friendly
- ✅ Include basic analytics/usage stats

## Revenue Potential

Based on the marketplace model:
- **Marketplace takes 20-30%** of each sale
- **Sellers keep 70-80%**
- **Featured placements** available for additional fee
- **Subscription revenue** shared monthly

Example:
- Tool priced at $10/month
- 100 subscribers = $1,000/month
- Seller keeps $700-800/month
- Marketplace earns $200-300/month
