# Launch Plan: micro-saasmarketplace.com

A 30-day action plan to launch and market your Micro-SaaS Marketplace.

## Timeline Overview

- **Week 1**: Pre-launch preparation
- **Week 2**: Soft launch & seeding
- **Week 3**: Public launch
- **Week 4**: Post-launch growth

---

## Week 1: Pre-Launch Preparation

### Day 1-2: Content & Assets

**Create:**
- [ ] Launch announcement blog post
- [ ] Social media graphics (Canva)
- [ ] Product screenshots/GIFs
- [ ] Founder story (for Product Hunt)
- [ ] Email templates
- [ ] Press kit (optional)

**Content Ideas:**
- "Why We Built a Marketplace for Micro-SaaS Tools"
- "The Problem with Current Tool Marketplaces"
- "Introducing Micro-SaaS Marketplace"

### Day 3-4: Seed Tools

**Goal: Get 10-15 tools listed**

**How:**
1. List your own tools (if you have any)
2. Reach out to friends/network
3. Offer free listings to early adopters
4. List free/open-source tools (with permission)

**Tools to Seed:**
- At least 2-3 per category
- Mix of free and paid
- Variety of use cases

### Day 5-6: Social Media Setup

**Create/Update:**
- [ ] Twitter/X account (@MicroSaaSMarket or similar)
- [ ] LinkedIn company page
- [ ] Instagram (optional)
- [ ] Product Hunt maker profile
- [ ] Indie Hackers profile

**Content:**
- Bio/description
- Profile images
- Cover images
- First few posts (build anticipation)

### Day 7: Community Outreach Prep

**Prepare:**
- [ ] List of communities to post in
- [ ] Personal messages to friends/network
- [ ] Email list (if you have one)
- [ ] Product Hunt launch scheduled
- [ ] Hacker News post ready

---

## Week 2: Soft Launch & Seeding

### Day 8-10: Soft Launch

**Who to Tell:**
- Close friends
- Early supporters
- Potential sellers
- Small creator communities

**Goals:**
- Get feedback
- Fix any bugs
- Build initial user base
- Get first reviews

**Channels:**
- Personal emails
- DMs to network
- Small Discord/Slack communities
- Twitter to small following

### Day 11-14: Seller Outreach

**Reach Out to:**
- Indie Hackers makers
- Product Hunt makers
- Twitter SaaS builders
- Your network

**Pitch:**
- "We're launching a marketplace for micro-SaaS tools"
- "Free listing for early adopters"
- "Get discovered by creators"
- "15% fee (vs 30% elsewhere)"

**Goal:** 5-10 more tools

---

## Week 3: Public Launch

### Day 15: Product Hunt Launch

**Timing:** 12:01 AM PST (Tuesday-Thursday best)

**Preparation:**
- [ ] Product Hunt submission ready
- [ ] Supporters ready to upvote
- [ ] Comments prepared
- [ ] Monitor all day

**Post-Launch:**
- Engage with every comment
- Thank upvoters
- Share on social media
- Update throughout the day

### Day 16: Hacker News

**Post:** "Show HN: Micro-SaaS Marketplace for Creator Tools"

**Best Time:** Weekday morning (PST)

**Engage:**
- Answer questions
- Be humble
- Accept feedback
- Don't be defensive

### Day 17: Social Media Blitz

**Twitter/X:**
- Launch thread (10-15 tweets)
- Tell your story
- Show features
- Ask for support

**LinkedIn:**
- Professional announcement
- Founder story
- Value proposition

**Reddit:**
- r/SaaS
- r/entrepreneur
- r/indiebiz
- r/startups

**Indie Hackers:**
- Launch post
- Engage in comments

### Day 18-19: Email & Outreach

**Email:**
- Newsletter subscribers (if any)
- Personal network
- Potential partners
- Bloggers/writers

**Outreach:**
- SaaS newsletters
- Creator economy newsletters
- Tech blogs
- Podcast hosts

### Day 20-21: Community Engagement

**Engage:**
- Respond to all comments
- Thank supporters
- Answer questions
- Collect feedback

**Content:**
- Behind-the-scenes posts
- Early user stories
- Tool spotlights

## Week 4: Post-Launch Growth

### Day 22-24: Content Creation

**Create:**
- Blog post: "Launch Week Recap"
- Blog post: "Top 10 Tools for Creators"
- Social content: Tool spotlights
- Email newsletter (if you have list)

**SEO:**
- Optimize tool pages
- Add meta descriptions
- Internal linking
- Category pages

### Day 25-26: Seller Acquisition Push

**Focus:**
- More seller outreach
- Improve onboarding
- Seller success stories
- Featured seller program

**Channels:**
- Indie Hackers
- Twitter
- Direct emails
- Community posts

### Day 27-28: Buyer Acquisition

**Focus:**
- Creator communities
- Content marketing
- SEO optimization
- Social media engagement

**Content:**
- "Tools for [specific creator type]"
- Comparison content
- How-to guides
- Tool reviews

### Day 29-30: Analysis & Planning

**Review:**
- What worked?
- What didn't?
- User feedback
- Metrics

**Plan:**
- Next month strategy
- Content calendar
- Growth initiatives
- Feature improvements

---

## Common Mistakes to Avoid

1. **Launching too early** - Make sure site works!
2. **No tools listed** - Need content for buyers
3. **Ignoring feedback** - Listen and iterate
4. **Spamming communities** - Provide value
5. **Giving up too soon** - Growth takes time
